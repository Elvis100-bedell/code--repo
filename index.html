Frequently Asked Questions

You May Ask

What is SMAC?
SMAC is the acronym of the ever potent Students' Movement for Academy Change, a Student political party based on NOAH'S ARK Campus.
When and why was SMAC founded?
SMAC was founded on March 17, 2014 to provide a sound, responsible and tolerant leadership for Noah's Ark student populace. irrespective of tribes, ethic, economic of religious background is indispensable to the goals of the party.
What is the Motto of SIM?
The Motto of SMAC is "Winners Never Quit"
What is the symbol of SMAC?
The circle symbolizes equality. The stars represent Completed solidarity of all races of never Quitter students.the white color clearly indicates that the struggle for the students and good governance is endless.
What are the colors of SMAC?
The colors of SMAC are brown and white.
Brown: The black symbolizes the magnificent Noah's Ark own students based oriented of our party.
What does SMAC believe in?
SMAC is committed to democratic princeples values, and practices participatory democracy is our guide post in reaching decision of far efforts. SMAC does not believe in un-necessary issues, tribalism, corruption, bad governess , yea our PARTY.
What is the philosophy of SMAC?
The philosophy of SMAC is centered on Pragmatism. Pragmatism is one of the founding pillars of American democracy. it is the process of making decisions that are practical or workable with respect to the various circumstances on ground.
Who are the Pragmatists?
In all civilize environment including Noah's Art pragmatists are the democrats, patriots, fair income earners, and agents of positive change. the conscious minded boys and girls who believe in bridging the gap that exist between the haves and have by advocating that everyone be given equal opportunity for achievement and advancement in society.
Who is eligible to become a member of SMAC?
Any bonafide and law-abiding student of Noah's Ark is eligible to become a member of SMAC.
How does one become a member of SMAC?
To become a member of SMAC, you must be law-abiding student. SMAC membership form are available free of charge apply for membership online or get a hard copy from any of our members.
